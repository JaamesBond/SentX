# Project Roadmap

## Overview

This roadmap outlines the **planned evolution** of the RobotLab OT/ICS Security Platform.
The project follows a structured, phase-based approach.

---

## Phase 1 – Design & Planning ✅

**Status:** Complete  
**Outcome:**
- Architecture defined
- Detection strategy mapped
- Framework alignment completed
- Pyramid of Pain fully planned

---

## Phase 2 – Core Infrastructure

**Focus:**
- Network segmentation
- Zeek & Suricata deployment
- Wazuh manager and agents

**Deliverables:**
- Baseline visibility
- Stable data ingestion
- Initial detections (L1–L2)

---

## Phase 3 – OT Protocol Awareness

**Focus:**
- Modbus TCP analysis
- OPC UA monitoring
- Robot-specific behaviors

**Deliverables:**
- OT-specific anomaly detection
- Protocol misuse alerts

---

## Phase 4 – Threat Intelligence Integration

**Focus:**
- CTI feed ingestion
- Indicator correlation
- APT context enrichment

**Deliverables:**
- Context-rich alerts
- Attribution-ready detections

---

## Phase 5 – Threat Hunting & TTPs

**Focus:**
- Multi-stage attack detection
- Hypothesis-driven hunts
- MITRE ATT&CK mapping

**Deliverables:**
- Level 6 Pyramid of Pain coverage
- Hunting playbooks

---

## Phase 6 – Visualization & Reporting

**Focus:**
- Dashboards
- Coverage matrices
- Analyst workflows

**Deliverables:**
- Operational visibility
- Academic presentation-ready output

---

## Guiding Principle

> **Accuracy over noise. Behavior over indicators. Safety over speed.**
